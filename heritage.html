BEST TIME TO VISIT MYSURU:

March to May: Summers in Mysore are warm with the temperature varying between 22°C and 39°C. The days are bright and sunny and the evenings are quite cool and pleasant so you can easily carry on with all tourist activities. May is usually the hottest month of the year here.

June to September: Monsoon months in Mysore experience medium to heavy rains and this is a beautiful time to witness the lush green surroundings. Night time tends to get quite cool after the rains so it’s a good idea to carry a light jacket.
