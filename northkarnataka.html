Must Places to Visit:


01. Badami Caves

  • The temples are about 88 miles (142 km) east of Belagavi (IATA Code: IXT), and 87 miles (140 km) northwest of Hampi. The Malaprabha River is 3 miles (4.8 km) away. The cave temples are 14 miles (23 km) from the UNESCO world heritage site Pattadakal and 22 miles (35 km) from Aihole.
  • Time taken to visit Badami Caves 2 to 3 hours.
      • Entry Fee:
  • Rs10 per person for Indian Natinals
  • Rs100 per person for Foreign Nationals
  • Entry free for childen up to 15 years


02. Pattadakal

  • Location: Bagalkot district, Karnataka
  • Nearest city: Badami
  • There is an entry fee of Rs. 30 for entering Pattadakal monuments and temples. You will have to pay extra for camera.
  • mallikarjuna temple, pattadakal remains open form 06:00 AM - 06:00 PM

05. Bidar Fort

  • Timings : 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM.
  • Time Required : 2-3 hrs.
  • Entry Fee : No entry fee.
  • the best time to visit Bidar Fort is between October and March as the weather remains pretty pleasant over there.


06. Gol Gumbaz, Bijapur

  • Entry Fees: Indian visitors- Rs 15 per head, Forign visitors- Rs 200 per head and Children up to 15 years of age- No Fees.
  • Visiting Time: all seven days of week, 10am to 5pm.
  • Visiting Duration: Since there are lots of things to be seen inside the tomb, it may be easy to take 1 to 2 hours to finish your visit at the Gol Gumbaz.


07. Kudala Sangama

  • Distance (From Alampur Bus Station): 1 Kms
  • Trip Duration (Including Travel): 30 Mins
  • Timings: The temple is open from 5:00 AM to 12:30 PM and from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM.


08. Saundatti Yellamma Temple

  • Goddess Renuka and a pilgrimage destination located about 5 kilometres (3.1 mi) away from the town of Saundatti
  • Timings to visit temple is from 6.30am to 4.30pm in morning and 6.30pm to 10pm iin the evening.


09. Narasimha Jharni

  • The ancient temple is excavated in a 300 m tunnel under the Manichoola hill range situated at around 4.8 km from the city.
  • Trip Duration (Including Travel): 1-2 Hours
  • Visitors need to go through a tunner with 4-5 feet deep water. Male pilgrims need to remove shirt.
  • Timings: 7 AM to 6 PM.
